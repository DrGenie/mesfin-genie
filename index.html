About Me

Dr Mesfin Genie is a Lecturer in Health Economics at the University of Newcastle and a Measurement & Regulatory Science Fellow at Duke University. His research applies discrete choice experiments to inform patient-centred policy, focusing on vaccine mandate evaluation, equity-efficiency trade-offs, and behavioural interventions. He has secured over $1.2 M in competitive funding and published extensively in top journals.
