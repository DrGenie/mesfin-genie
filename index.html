About Me

Dr Mesfin Genie is a Lecturer in Health Economics at the University of Newcastle and a Measurement & Regulatory Science Fellow at Duke University. He applies discrete choice experiments to inform patient-centred policy, focusing on vaccine mandate evaluation, equity-efficiency trade-offs, and behavioural interventions. He has secured over $1.2 million in competitive funding and published extensively in leading journals.

Education

  • MSc Health Economics & Management, University of Bologna (2015)
  • MSc Economics, Addis Ababa University (2010)
